KRABBY PATTY

Description

FROM SPONGEBOB SERIES

OK, so maybe it isn't the exact thing, but my kids seem to like it...

although Mr. Krabs doesn't reveal the exact formula,

he does give a hint that a secret ingredient is plankton!

INGREDIENTS

  • Tomato
  • 1 pound crabmeat
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • Hamburger buns (whatever you like) lightly toasted
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • American Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Hot sauce/Tabasco sauce to taste
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 quart oil for frying

ASSEMBLE AS FOLLOWS

  1. Bottom bun
  2. Patty
  3. Ketchup
  4. Mustard
  5. Cheese (or you can melt over patties)
  6. Two pickle slices
  7. Lettuce
  8. Tomato
  9. Onion Rings
  10. Top bun
